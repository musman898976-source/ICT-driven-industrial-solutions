Engineered ICT Solutions

1. Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT)

Don't just collect data; contextualize it. Our framework connects legacy PLC systems to cloud analytics via MQTT protocols, enabling real-time OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness) tracking.

2. Cybersecurity for OT

Industrial systems are vulnerable. We implement Perimeter Defense-in-Depth and air-gapped backups to ensure that a ransomware attack on the IT side doesn't stop the assembly line.

3. AI-Driven Demand Forecasting

Eliminate the "Bullwhip Effect" in supply chains. Our AI models analyze historical throughput and external market trends to optimize procurement schedules.
