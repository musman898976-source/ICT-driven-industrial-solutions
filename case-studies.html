Case Study: Mid-Sized Textile Mill

Before ICT Implementation

  • Manual logbooks for machine uptime (15% error rate).
  • 22% waste in dye chemicals due to manual mixing errors.
  • Average downtime: 48 hours/month.

After ICT Integration (Phase 1)

  • IoT Flow Meters: Waste reduced by 18% in 3 months.
  • Digital Twin Dashboard: Real-time visibility reduced downtime to 12 hours/month.
  • Annual Savings: $420,000 USD.
